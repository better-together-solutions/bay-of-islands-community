About the Bay of Islands

The Bay of Islands is a breathtaking region on the west coast of Newfoundland, Canada. With Corner Brook as its largest social and economic center, the area offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and modern amenities.

Nestled amidst picturesque hills and surrounded by pristine waters, the Bay of Islands is an outdoor enthusiast's paradise. From hiking and kayaking to fishing and wildlife spotting, there's something for everyone to enjoy.
